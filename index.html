About Cross River State

Nestled in southeastern Nigeria, Cross River State is a vibrant tapestry of culture, nature, and adventure. Renowned for its lush rainforests and diverse wildlife, it’s home to the breathtaking Cross River National Park, where you can experience rich biodiversity and stunning landscapes. The state’s capital, Calabar, pulses with history and festivity, particularly during the famous Calabar Carnival, dubbed "Africa's Biggest Street Party." With its warm hospitality, delicious local cuisine, and unique traditions, Cross River State offers a captivating glimpse into Nigeria's rich heritage and natural beauty. Whether you're trekking through scenic trails or enjoying lively cultural festivals, Cross River is a destination that promises unforgettable experiences.
